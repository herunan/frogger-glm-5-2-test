HOW TO PLAY

Get your frog across the road and river to safety. Fill all 5 home lily pads to win the daily challenge.

🛣️
Road: Dodge cars and trucks. Touch one and you lose a life.
🌊
River: Ride logs and turtles. Don't fall in the water!
🐢
Turtles: Some dive periodically — don't get caught underwater.
⏱️
Time: Each crossing has 60 seconds. Be quick!
🏠
Homes: Land on a lily pad to score. Miss and you hit the bushes.

Everyone gets the same level each day. Come back tomorrow for a new challenge!
